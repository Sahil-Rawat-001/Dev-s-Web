Lemon Juice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lemon
  • Soda/Water
  • Black salt
  • Sugar
  • Ice Cubes

Instructions

  1. Add Two Glass of water/soda and 4 teable Spoon of Sugar.
  2. Add some black salt into it according to your taste.
  3. Extract Lemon juice and add into it
  4. Then mix the solution well.
  5. Add some Ice cubes.
  6. Enjoy the drink.
